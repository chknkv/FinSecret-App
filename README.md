### FinSecret Mobile App

**FinSecret** is a modern, secure, and cross-platform **FinTech Startup** mobile application designed for personal finance management. Built with the cutting-edge **Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)** technology stack, it provides a unified and seamless user experience on both **Android** and **iOS** devices.

## ðŸš€ Purpose & Value Proposition

The core value of **FinSecret** is to provide **clarity and control** over your personal economy. It transforms financial anxiety into confidence through precise tracking and future calculation.

*   **Financial Clarity:** Easily record income and expenses to keep a digital ledger of your finances.
*   **Smart Dashboard:** Visualize financial trends (Trending Up/Down) and summary cards at a glance.
*   **Wallet Management:** Manage multiple wallets and view consolidated balances.
*   **Security First:** Integrated **Passcode/PIN protection** ensures that sensitive financial data remains private.
*   **Cross-Platform:** A single codebase delivering native-performance UI on both mobile platforms.
